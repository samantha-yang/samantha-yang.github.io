Beach Trip

June 25th, 2022

Today I went to the beach with my friends. It was so fun. The first thing we did
was set up our umbrella and towels on the sand. After that, we put on sunscreen
because it was very sunny that day. We swam in the water for hours. After that,
we were hungry so we decided to have a barbeque. We grilled steak and chicken
wings. We also had fruit salad and lots of cool drinks. It would be so fun to do this
again!

Coffee Outing

March 10th, 2022

My friends invited me to a coffee outing today. We decided to try out the new
cafe that everyone had been talking about. I had seen a bit about it on social media,
so I was excited to try out their coffee. When we got there, there was a bit of a line since it was the afternoon. However, the wait was well worth it. I got a cute bear design
on the top of my latte. I took a picture of it and posted it to social media. Everyone
seemed to really enjoy their drinks. The vibe of the cafe was also chill.

I would recommend you to go try it if you have the opportunity to. I definitely need
to try again to taste their other drinks!
